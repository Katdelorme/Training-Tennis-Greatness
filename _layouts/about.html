Meet Burd

Liburd Paul Germain, best known as Coach Burd, has been a tennis instructor for over fifteen years. He has taught in multiple countries including St-Tropez-France, Denmark, Valencia-Spain, and Florence-Italy. Currently he is teaching in his hometown of Miami, Florida at the Miami Shores Country Club. He speaks English, Creole and French and is able to communicate in Spanish.
